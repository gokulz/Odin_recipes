Green Bean Casserole

Green Bean Casserole is American baked Dish consisting primarily of green beans, cream of mushroom soup, and french fried onions.

It is a popular side dish for Thanksgiving dinners in the United States and has been described as iconic.

Description

How to make Green Bean Casserole.

Ingredients

  • Green beans
  • Canned soup
  • Cheese
  • French-fried onions
  • Optional ingredients

Steps

  1. Mix the beans and soup in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave until warm.
  2. Stir in half the cheese. Microwave until melted and well-blended.
  3. Transfer to a prepared baking dish. Top with fried onions and remaining cheese.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is melted and the onions are brown.
