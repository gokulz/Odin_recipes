What is Dessert?

Dessert is a Course that concludes a Meal.The course is consists of Sweet foods, Such as Confections.

The Term Dessert can apply to many Confections such as,

  • Biscuits
  • Cokkies
  • Cakes
  • Ice Creams
  • pies
  • Macroons

Description

There are many types of Desserts here we can see about how to make mug cake.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp self-raising flour
  • 4 tbsp caster sugar
  • 2tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 medium egg
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil or sunflower oil
  • a few drops of vanilla essence
  • 2 tbsp chocolate chips, nuts, or raisins

Method

Step 1
- Add 4 tbsp self-raising flour,caster sugar and cocoapowder to largest mug you have.
Step 2
- Add 1 medium egg and mix in as much as you can.
Step 3
- Add the milk, vegetable oil and few drops of vannila essence and mix all the ingredients.
Step 4
- Centre your mug in the middle of the microwave oven and cook in high 1½ - 2 mins, or until it has stopped rising.
